# Wiley / Advanced Materials Pack

## Use For

Advanced Materials, Advanced Functional Materials, Small, Advanced Science, Angewandte Chemie, Chemistry - A European Journal, and related Wiley or Wiley-VCH journals.

## Official Sources To Verify

- Wiley Author Services: https://authors.wiley.com/
- Wiley author guidelines and manuscript preparation pages.
- Target journal page on Wiley Online Library or Advanced Online Library.
- Journal-specific templates, article type pages, and graphical/TOC entry rules.

## Default Manuscript Map

- Title.
- Authors and affiliations.
- Corresponding author email.
- Abstract.
- Keywords.
- Main text with journal-appropriate headings.
- Experimental Section or Methods, depending on journal and article type.
- Supporting Information statement.
- Acknowledgements.
- Conflict of Interest.
- Data Availability Statement when required.
- References.

## Formatting Checks

- Verify whether the article type is Research Article, Communication, Review, Progress Report, Full Paper, or another journal-specific category.
- Check whether the journal requires a table-of-contents entry, graphical abstract, keywords, or biographies.
- Confirm reference style, display-item limits, figure preparation rules, and supporting information naming.
- For materials journals, check figure resolution, scale bars, microscopy image requirements, and chemical/materials nomenclature.

## Do Not Assume

- Do not apply Advanced Materials limits to all Wiley-VCH journals.
- Do not invent TOC figures, graphical abstracts, author biographies, or supporting information statements.
- Do not treat Wiley "free format" initial submission as final accepted-manuscript formatting.
