# IEEE Pack

## Use For

IEEE journals, transactions, letters, magazines, and conference-style articles.

## Official Sources To Verify

- IEEE Author Center: https://ieeeauthorcenter.ieee.org/
- IEEE article templates and target publication author instructions.
- Target journal or conference page.

## Default Manuscript Map

- Title.
- Authors and affiliations.
- Abstract.
- Index Terms.
- Main text, commonly Introduction, Methods/System Model, Results, Discussion, Conclusion.
- Appendices when needed.
- Acknowledgement.
- References.
- Author biographies and photos when required by publication stage.

## Formatting Checks

- Verify whether the target uses journal, transaction, letter, magazine, or conference template requirements.
- Check whether the initial submission must be single-column or two-column.
- Confirm IEEE reference style, figure/table caption placement, equation numbering, and Index Terms.
- Check whether author biographies are required only after acceptance.

## Do Not Assume

- Do not force two-column formatting unless the official template or submission stage requires it.
- Do not invent Index Terms or author biographies.
- Do not apply conference rules to journals or transactions without verification.
