# Cell Press Pack

## Use For

Cell, Cell Reports, Matter, Joule, iScience, Trends journals, and other Cell Press journals.

## Official Sources To Verify

- Cell Press journal author page, for example: https://www.cell.com/cell/authors
- Cell Press STAR Methods guidance where applicable: https://www.cell.com/star-authors-guide
- Target journal article type and formatting page.

## Default Manuscript Map

- Title.
- Authors and affiliations.
- Lead contact and corresponding author.
- Summary.
- Keywords.
- Highlights when required.
- eTOC blurb or graphical abstract when required.
- Introduction.
- Results.
- Discussion.
- Limitations of the study when required.
- STAR Methods or Experimental Procedures.
- Resource availability, including lead contact, materials availability, and data/code availability where required.
- Acknowledgements.
- Author contributions.
- Declaration of interests.
- References.

## Formatting Checks

- Verify whether the target journal requires Highlights, graphical abstract, eTOC blurb, STAR Methods, or resource availability subsections.
- Check whether methods are structured under STAR Methods headings.
- Check declaration naming: "Declaration of interests" is common in Cell Press workflows, but verify journal-specific wording.
- Confirm reference style and whether article titles should be included.

## Do Not Assume

- Do not force STAR Methods into article types or journals that do not require it.
- Do not invent lead contact, materials availability, data/code availability, or declaration text.
- Do not create graphical abstract text if the user has not supplied content.
