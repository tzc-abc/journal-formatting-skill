# MDPI Pack

## Use For

MDPI journals such as Materials, Sensors, Applied Sciences, Nanomaterials, Molecules, and other MDPI titles.

## Official Sources To Verify

- MDPI Information for Authors: https://www.mdpi.com/authors
- Target journal instructions, template, article type, and special issue page.

## Default Manuscript Map

- Title.
- Authors and affiliations.
- Abstract.
- Keywords.
- Introduction.
- Materials and Methods.
- Results.
- Discussion.
- Conclusions.
- Patents when relevant.
- Supplementary Materials.
- Author Contributions.
- Funding.
- Institutional Review Board Statement when relevant.
- Informed Consent Statement when relevant.
- Data Availability Statement.
- Conflicts of Interest.
- References.

## Formatting Checks

- Verify the target journal template and article type.
- Check whether the journal expects numbered sections and mandatory back-matter statements.
- Confirm reference style, figure/table placement, supplementary materials, and ethics statements.
- Check whether special issue instructions add requirements.

## Do Not Assume

- Do not fabricate IRB, informed consent, data availability, or conflict statements.
- Do not keep mandatory MDPI back matter if the target journal page says it is not applicable.
- Do not assume one MDPI journal template covers every article type.
