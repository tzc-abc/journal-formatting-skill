# Springer Nature / BMC Pack

## Use For

Springer journals, Springer Nature journals, BMC journals, and Palgrave journals outside the Nature Portfolio workflow.

## Official Sources To Verify

- Springer Nature author resources: https://www.springernature.com/gp/authors
- Target journal "Submission Guidelines" page.
- BMC article type, reporting standards, data availability, ethics, and competing interests pages where applicable.

## Default Manuscript Map

- Title page.
- Abstract.
- Keywords.
- Main text using journal-specific structure.
- Methods or Materials and Methods when required.
- Results.
- Discussion.
- Conclusions when required.
- Abbreviations when required.
- Declarations section.
- Ethics approval and consent statements when relevant.
- Consent for publication when relevant.
- Availability of data and materials.
- Competing interests.
- Funding.
- Authors' contributions.
- Acknowledgements.
- References.

## Formatting Checks

- Verify exact journal article type and Springer/BMC submission guideline page.
- Check whether declarations must be grouped under a "Declarations" heading.
- Check ethics, consent, clinical trial registration, data/materials availability, and reporting guideline requirements.
- Confirm reference style, figure/table location, and supplementary information naming.

## Do Not Assume

- Do not apply BMC biomedical declaration requirements to every Springer journal without verification.
- Do not fabricate ethics, consent, trial registration, data, or materials availability statements.
- Do not treat Nature Portfolio rules as the same as Springer or BMC rules.
