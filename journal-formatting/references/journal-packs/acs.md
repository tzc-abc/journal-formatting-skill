# ACS Publications Pack

## Use For

Journal of the American Chemical Society, ACS Nano, ACS Applied Materials & Interfaces, ACS Energy Letters, ACS Catalysis, Nano Letters, and other ACS journals.

## Official Sources To Verify

- ACS Publish author resources: https://publish.acs.org/
- Target ACS journal "Author Guidelines" page.
- ACS manuscript templates, Supporting Information guidance, and graphical abstract rules where applicable.

## Default Manuscript Map

- Title.
- Authors and affiliations.
- Corresponding author email.
- Abstract.
- Keywords when required.
- Introduction.
- Results and Discussion or journal-specific main sections.
- Experimental Section, Methods, or Supporting Information placement as required.
- Associated Content / Supporting Information.
- Author Information.
- Acknowledgements.
- References.

## Formatting Checks

- Verify article type and journal-specific author guideline page.
- Check whether TOC/graphical abstract, synopsis, or cover-art material is required.
- Confirm ACS reference style, author information, ORCID, and supporting information wording.
- Check compound/material naming, safety statements, crystallographic data, spectra, and data repository requirements when relevant.

## Do Not Assume

- Do not apply one ACS journal's article type limits to another ACS journal.
- Do not invent chemical safety, crystallographic, spectra, or data deposition statements.
- Do not rewrite references into ACS style without checking incomplete entries.
