# Science / AAAS Pack

## Use For

Science, Science Advances, Science Translational Medicine, Science Signaling, and other AAAS journals.

## Official Sources To Verify

- Science Information for Authors: https://www.science.org/content/page/science-information-authors
- Target AAAS journal "Information for Authors" page.
- AAAS editorial policies and data/materials availability requirements.

## Default Manuscript Map

- Title.
- Authors and affiliations.
- Abstract or one-sentence summary where required by article type.
- Main text using the journal's required section structure.
- References and notes.
- Acknowledgements.
- Funding.
- Author contributions.
- Competing interests.
- Data and materials availability.
- Supplementary materials list.

## Formatting Checks

- Verify article type, because Science-family formats differ strongly across Research Article, Report, Review, Policy Forum, Perspective, and other categories.
- Check whether the journal requires a structured data/materials availability statement.
- Confirm reference and notes style, supplementary material callouts, and figure/table placement rules.
- Check whether figures/tables are embedded for review or uploaded separately.

## Do Not Assume

- Do not apply Science flagship limits to Science Advances or other AAAS journals without verification.
- Do not invent availability statements for materials, code, data, or protocols.
- Do not omit supplementary material checks; AAAS submissions often depend on them.
