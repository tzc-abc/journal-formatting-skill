# PLOS Pack

## Use For

PLOS ONE, PLOS Biology, PLOS Medicine, PLOS Computational Biology, PLOS Genetics, and other PLOS journals.

## Official Sources To Verify

- PLOS author resources: https://plos.org/publish/
- Target PLOS journal submission guidelines.
- PLOS data availability, ethics, reporting guideline, and competing interests policies.

## Default Manuscript Map

- Title.
- Authors and affiliations.
- Abstract.
- Author summary when required by journal/article type.
- Introduction.
- Materials and Methods.
- Results.
- Discussion.
- Acknowledgements.
- References.
- Supporting information captions.
- Data availability statement.
- Funding statement.
- Competing interests statement.
- Ethics statement when relevant.

## Formatting Checks

- Verify target PLOS journal because structure and article-type requirements vary.
- Check data availability, funding, competing interests, and ethics statement requirements.
- Confirm figure/table callouts, supporting information captions, and reference style.
- Check reporting guideline and protocol requirements for clinical, animal, systematic review, or other regulated studies.

## Do Not Assume

- Do not invent data availability, funding, competing interests, ethics, or author summary text.
- Do not apply PLOS ONE structure to all PLOS journals.
- Do not omit supporting information caption checks.
